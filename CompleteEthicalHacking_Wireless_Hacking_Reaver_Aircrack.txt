PenTesting / Ethical Hacking Notes: 

- TOPIC: Wireless Hacking with Reaver and Aircrack

1. you can download aircrack with: 
"yum install aircrack-ng -y"
remember that -y will say yes to any prompts

2. to get reaver you will need to go to google code: 
https://code.google.com/archive/p/reaver-wps/downloads
Just grab latest 
This is also installed on Kali

3. Example REaver command: 
-i interface 
-b MAC address of the endpoint
-vv verbose logging 

reaver -i mon0 -b 00:90:4C:C1:AC:21 -vv -K 1
